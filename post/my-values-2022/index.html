My purpose and values, going into 2022

Fri, Dec 31, 2021

We’re purpose and value driven people. As much as our world seems to be logical and thoughtful, our perceptions deceive us. We are ruled by automatic and emotional guidance1.

Last year I started to consciously review what my values were2 and revisit them every week. It gave me a grounding point to make sure my emotions and automatic systems weren’t carrying me away from what is important.

This year I’m making them public. My most important values focus on relationships because, for me, the most positive human experiences come from other people. Therefore, it’s important that others know what I value so we can have the most successful relationships.

It’s an inconsequential drop in the universe of things and words. But it’s the drop that centers my life and hopefully allows others to orient around or with me so we can thrive together.

My purpose

Cultivate positive human experiences, starting with myself but not wholly for myself and with emphasis on others.

What I value

  1. Deep interpersonal relationships
  2. Honest and candid communication
  3. Contributing to society
  4. Work that is beneficial to our society (does not have to be directly beneficial)
  5. Fundamental, inalienable human rights
